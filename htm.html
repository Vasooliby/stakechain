How it works:

Staking

Crypto staking is a way to earn rewards by holding and locking a cryptocurrency to support a blockchain network's operations. Stakers, or validators, help validate transactions and secure the network by adding blocks to the blockchain. In return, they receive staking rewards in the form of additional cryptocurrency.
